The editing column appears when [editing](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Widgets/dxTreeList/Configuration/editing/) is allowed. The avaiable editing buttons depend on the [editing mode](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Widgets/dxTreeList/Configuration/editing/#mode).

The editing column is a type of buttons column. To customize it, set the column's [type](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Widgets/dxTreeList/Configuration/columns/#type) to *"buttons"* and specify the [other options](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Widgets/dxTreeList/Configuration/columns/).
