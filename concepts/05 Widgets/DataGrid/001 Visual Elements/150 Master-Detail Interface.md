The master-detail interface enables you to display additional information in the expandable section of a data row. The data row is called "master row", while the section is called "detail section".

![Total Summary](/Content/images/doc/17_2/DataGrid/MasterDetail.png)

To learn how to use this feature, refer to the [Master-Detail Interface](/Documentation/Guide/Widgets/DataGrid/Master-Detail_Interface/) topic.
