By default, group summary items are displayed in the header of the group row. You can change item location.

- Align items by columns within the group row.  
Use the [alignByColumn](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Widgets/dxDataGrid/Configuration/summary/groupItems/#alignByColumn) option of the group summary item that you need to align by the corresponding column.

- Place items to the group footer.  
Set the [showInGroupFooter](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Widgets/dxDataGrid/Configuration/summary/groupItems/#showInGroupFooter) option to *true*.

Whether you align group summary items by columns in the group row or in the group footer, you can specify a custom column for the group items. For this purpose, use the [showInColumn](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Widgets/dxDataGrid/Configuration/summary/groupItems/#showInColumn) option.
