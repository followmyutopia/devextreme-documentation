A palette is a set of colors that mix well with each other. Palettes are used to colorize the following widget elements:

- **BarGauge**: [bars](/Documentation/Guide/Widgets/BarGauge/Visual_Elements/#Bars), [bar labels](/Documentation/Guide/Widgets/BarGauge/Visual_Elements/#Labels)
- **Chart**: [series](/Documentation/Guide/Widgets/Chart/Series/Overview/), [series points](/Documentation/Guide/Widgets/Chart/Series_Points/Overview/), [series point labels](/Documentation/Guide/Widgets/Chart/Point_Labels/Overview/), [legend items](/Documentation/Guide/Widgets/Chart/Legend/Overview/)
- **CircularGauge**: [subvalue indicators](/Documentation/Guide/Widgets/CircularGauge/Visual_Elements/#Subvalue_Indicators), [range container](/Documentation/Guide/Widgets/CircularGauge/Visual_Elements/#Range_Container)
- **Funnel**: [funnel items](/Documentation/Guide/Widgets/Funnel/Funnel_Items/Overview/), [funnel item labels](/Documentation/Guide/Widgets/Funnel/Item_Labels/Overview/)
- **LinearGauge**: [subvalue indicators](/Documentation/Guide/Widgets/LinearGauge/Visual_Elements/#Subvalue_Indicators), [range container](/Documentation/Guide/Widgets/LinearGauge/Visual_Elements/#Range_Container)
- **PieChart**: [series](/Documentation/Guide/Widgets/PieChart/Series/Overview/), [series point labels](/Documentation/Guide/Widgets/PieChart/Point_Labels/Overview/), [legend items](/Documentation/Guide/Widgets/PieChart/Legend/Overview/)
- **PolarChart**: [series](/Documentation/Guide/Widgets/PolarChart/Visual_Elements/#Series), [series points](/Documentation/Guide/Widgets/PolarChart/Visual_Elements/#Series_Points), [series point labels](/Documentation/Guide/Widgets/PolarChart/Visual_Elements/#Series_Point_Labels), [legend items](/Documentation/Guide/Widgets/PolarChart/Visual_Elements/#Legend)
- **Sankey**: [nodes and links](/Documentation/Guide/Widgets/Sankey/Nodes_and_Links/Overview/), [node labels](/Documentation/Guide/Widgets/Sankey/Node_Labels/Overview/)
- **TreeMap**: [tiles](/Documentation/ApiReference/Data_Visualization_Widgets/dxTreeMap/Configuration/tile/), [tile groups](/Documentation/ApiReference/Data_Visualization_Widgets/dxTreeMap/Configuration/group/)
- **VectorMap**: [areas](/Documentation/Guide/Widgets/VectorMap/Visual_Elements/#Areas), [markers](/Documentation/Guide/Widgets/VectorMap/Visual_Elements/#Markers), [legend items](/Documentation/Guide/Widgets/VectorMap/Visual_Elements/#Legend)
