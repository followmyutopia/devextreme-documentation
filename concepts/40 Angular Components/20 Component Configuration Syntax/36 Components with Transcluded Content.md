Widgets like [Resizable](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Widgets/dxResizable/), [ScrollView](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Widgets/dxScrollView/), and [ValidationGroup](/Documentation/ApiReference/UI_Widgets/dxValidationGroup/) allow you to declare their content directly in the markup. The following is an example with **ScrollView**:

    <!--HTML-->
    <dx-scroll-view>
        <div>Some scrollable content</div>
    </dx-scroll-view>
