To clear grouping options and show data items without grouping pass **null** to the **groupData** function.

Add the "clear" button to *markup.html* and specify the **click** event handler for this button. Insert the **groupData** function call to the body of the **click** event handler. Pass **null** to the **groupData** function to clear grouping options.
