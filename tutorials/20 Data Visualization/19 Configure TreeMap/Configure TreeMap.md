The **TreeMap** widget displays flat or hierarchical data using nested rectangles, called "tiles", that are sized and ordered by a quantitative variable. This tutorial details how to configure **TreeMap**. Following the steps of this tutorial, you will learn to provide data for a treemap and give it a distinctive look that delivers your data the best way possible. Data for this tutorial is taken from [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_countries_by_vehicles_per_capita).
