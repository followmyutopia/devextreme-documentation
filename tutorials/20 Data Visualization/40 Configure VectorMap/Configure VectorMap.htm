This manual provides detailed instructions on how to configure the dxVectorMap widget. Having performed the actions referred in this topic, you will learn how to provide data for map areas and markers, choose the appropriate position of the map, customize map elements and interact with the map. These features will be explained on a simple example. As a result, you will get the dxVectorMap widget that displays the map of the United States with the capitals of 10 most populous states indicated on it.
