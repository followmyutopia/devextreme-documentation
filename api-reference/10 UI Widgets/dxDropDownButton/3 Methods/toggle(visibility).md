<!--shortDescription-->
Opens or closes the drop-down menu, depending on the argument.
<!--/shortDescription-->

<!--paramName1-->visibility<!--/paramName1-->
<!--paramType1-->Boolean<!--/paramType1-->
<!--paramDescription1-->
Pass **true** to open the drop-down menu; **false** to close.
<!--/paramDescription1-->

<!--returnType-->Promise<void><!--/returnType-->
<!--returnDescription-->
A Promise that is resolved when the drop-down menu is opened or closed. It is a [native Promise](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/Promise) or a [jQuery.Promise](http://api.jquery.com/Types/#Promise) when you use jQuery.
<!--/returnDescription-->
