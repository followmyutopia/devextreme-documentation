<!--id-->dxDropDownButton.toggle(visibility)<!--/id-->
===================================================================
===================================================================

<!--shortDescription-->
Opens or closes the drop-down menu, depending on the argument.
<!--/shortDescription-->

<!--paramName1-->visibility<!--/paramName1-->
<!--paramType1-->Boolean<!--/paramType1-->
<!--paramDescription1-->
Pass **true** to open the drop-down menu; **false** to close.
<!--/paramDescription1-->

<!--returnType-->Promise<void><!--/returnType-->
<!--returnDescription-->
A Promise that is resolved when the drop-down menu is opened or closed. It is a <a href="https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/Promise" target="_blank">native Promise</a> or a <a href="http://api.jquery.com/Types/#Promise" target="_blank">jQuery.Promise</a> when you use jQuery.
<!--/returnDescription-->
