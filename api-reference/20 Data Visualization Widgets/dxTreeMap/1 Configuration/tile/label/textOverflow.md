##### shortDescription
Specifies what to do with labels that overflow their tiles after applying [wordWrap](/Documentation/ApiReference/Data_Visualization_Widgets/dxTreeMap/Configuration/tile/label/#wordWrap): hide, truncate them and display an ellipsis, or do nothing.
