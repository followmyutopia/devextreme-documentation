This method hides a series at runtime. To specify a series hidden at design-time, set the **series** | [visible](/Documentation/ApiReference/Data_Visualization_Widgets/dxPolarChart/Configuration/commonSeriesSettings/#visible) option to *false*.

[note] When the series is invisible, the marker of its [legend item](/Documentation/Guide/Widgets/PolarChart/Visual_Elements/#Legend) is faded.
