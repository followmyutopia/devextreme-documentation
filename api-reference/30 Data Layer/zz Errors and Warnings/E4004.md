<!--shortDescription-->
Occurs when the [thenby()](/Documentation/ApiReference/Data_Layer/Query/Methods/#thenBygetter) method is called before the [sortby()](/Documentation/ApiReference/Data_Layer/Query/Methods/#sortBygetter) method.
<!--/shortDescription-->

<!--fullDescription-->
The **thenby()** method can only follow the **sortBy(getter)**, **sortBy(getter, desc)**, **thenBy(getter, desc)**, or another **thenBy(getter)** method.
<!--/fullDescription-->
