##### shortDescription
Specifies what to do with the annotation's [text](/api-reference/_hidden/dxChartCommonAnnotationConfig/text.md '/Documentation/ApiReference/Data_Visualization_Widgets/dxChart/Configuration/annotations/#text') when it overflows the allocated space after applying [wordWrap](/api-reference/_hidden/dxChartCommonAnnotationConfig/wordWrap.md '/Documentation/ApiReference/Data_Visualization_Widgets/dxChart/Configuration/annotations/#wordWrap'): hide, truncate it and display an ellipsis, or do nothing.
